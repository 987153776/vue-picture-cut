Vue-picture-cut

Image clipping tool based on Vue and Typescript. Feature：Scale, flip, rotate, edge check, rectangle clip, ellipse clip. 基于vue和typescript的图片剪裁工具。功能：缩放、翻折、旋转、边缘校验、矩形剪裁、任意(椭)圆剪裁
